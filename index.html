About Me

Currently leading design at Atlassian on the Growth team as an IC Director. Before that, I worked at Statsig as Head of Design, Director. My career started at Facebook where I successfully launched highly impactful product features, reaching one of the fastest promotion track and exceptional ratings at the company.

As a designer, I take a very pragmatic and data-informed approach to problem solving. I like to understand key business goals, metrics that the team cares about and ultimately thinking how they shape the roadmap and the reasons/purposes behind my initiatives. I know how to be scrappy and flexible but I also know when to button up and really think through the design solutions. trip to dubai

As a software developer with a growing focus on user-centered digital experiences, I've had the opportunity to build solutions that connect real users to real outcomes — even in environments where traditional internet access isn’t guaranteed. One standout project I contributed to was the NESTLÉ IDEAL Trip to Dubai Promo, where I worked as a USSD developer to design and implement an engagement flow that enabled thousands of consumers to participate effortlessly via mobile. This experience reinforced my passion for building accessible, reliable, and scalable technology that meets users where they are — whether on the web or through mobile networks.


At the heart of my work is a commitment to simplicity, clarity, and seamless interaction. From integrating USSD logic with backend systems to supporting real-time monitoring dashboards, I enjoy transforming complex processes into intuitive user journeys. This project not only strengthened my technical skills across USSD architecture, backend logic, and campaign integration, but also taught me the value of cross-functional collaboration and thoughtful design. As I continue to grow as a developer, I’m excited to apply these lessons to new challenges and build impactful digital experiences across platforms.

Hotel Management UI System

As a software developer committed to creating clear, intuitive, and user-focused digital solutions, I designed and developed a fully functional hotel management system that demonstrates my ability to turn real operational needs into practical, responsive interfaces. This project allowed me to combine front-end development, UI/UX principles, and structured problem-solving to build a system that helps users navigate bookings, manage rooms, and interact with information in a clean, efficient way. It strengthened my understanding of how thoughtful design and smart functionality work together to enhance user experience.


What I enjoyed most about this project was translating complex workflows—such as room validation, navigation, and user actions—into a seamless, easy-to-use interface. By applying modular design, JavaScript interactivity, and structured project organisation, I created a foundation that can be extended into a full-stack application with backend and database integration. This project solidified my ability to approach challenges iteratively, apply Agile principles, and continuously refine solutions based on user feedback and technical insight. It remains a key example of how I bring together design thinking, front-end engineering, and practical problem-solving to build meaningful, user-centered applications.
